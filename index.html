Index Design Process

Step 1: Define Objectives

Identify specific investment objectives and market segments that the index will track.

Step 2: Universe Selection

Establish selection criteria and identify the universe of securities eligible for the index.

Step 3: Weighting Methodology

Develop appropriate weighting methodology based on market capitalization, book value, or other factors.

Step 4: Maintenance Rules

Establish clear rules for adding, removing, and rebalancing index constituents.

Step 5: Calculation and Backtesting

Calculate historical performance and test the methodology's robustness under different market conditions.

Step 6: Implementation and Monitoring

Launch the index and establish ongoing monitoring systems to ensure quality and performance.
